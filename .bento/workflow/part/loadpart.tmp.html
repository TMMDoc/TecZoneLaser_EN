There are three different ways to load a part in Part Workflow.

  1. Click the open icon and select a part to import.

  1. Click on the File menu and select Open. Shortcut key ctrl+O.

  1. Open your file explorer (in which your part is saved), select the files and drag and drop into TecZone Laser.
