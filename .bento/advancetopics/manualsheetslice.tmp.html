TecZone Laser supports manual sheet slicing cuts. Just like automatic sheet slicing cuts, TecZone Laser will carefully manage the machine head at the sheet boundaries by locking the head position. The software can also detect if a remainder sheet is created by such a cut and can export the same. However, if an automatic remainder cut is also present, then the remainder sheet created by manual slices is ignored.

Manual sheet slicing cuts are supported even for non-rectangular sheets.

It is not necessary that a laser cut inserted on the sheet layout create a remainder sheet.

Snap points are provided to align the cuts perpendicular to the sheet boundary.
